My Blogs

A Few Withered Flowers

I wouldn't blame myself if I am fantasizing enough,
but it scares me to notice that the oceans which I considered deep
are shallower than the streams which exist.

It scares me enough how a few prioritised flowers withered so calmly
like they never existed.

Well I am just a human who longs to hold on to the beautiful moments.
It scares me enough like they slipped through my hands and I never noticed.

Now I have learned to let go,
Let go of those dull luring smiles
Which were never true.

— DT

Mellow-Spice

Its time to change seasons.
What an odd thing to say?

But if its too hard to even relate,
Its too hard to even grip.
And evenings find it hard to even hold on.

Its time to change seasons.
Pretty much never mattered when it did.
A lot of storms happened this season.
It wasn't healthy, but not harder than last.

Its time to change seasons,
Time to touch the grass, and
Be me again.

What an odd thing to say?

— Debolina Tapadar

My Poems

The Bloom

She is not an illusion you want to assume,
She is not the sky you want to look up to,
She is just another phase you needed to face.

Well, may not be the most beautiful, but she was happy to stay.
She lived in those seconds which she assumed were happy.

She never needed too much,
She was just happy.

Even if the star was gone from her life,
She would rather remain same.

Oh the world never knew her,
She was me and I was her.
Lost through those scars which were supposed to be mine.

— Debo
